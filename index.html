Hi, I am Brianna Wimer :)

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Joint Ph.D. program between the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the Department of Psychology at the University of Notre Dame. My advisors are Ronald Metoyer and Bradley Gibson. I am a member of the HCI Lab.

My research interests include understanding, designing, and developing technologies for mental health and people with cognitive disabilities. My research is interdisciplinary and incorporates human-computer interaction, assistive technology, cognitive-aware systems, and digital mental health
