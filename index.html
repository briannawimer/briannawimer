Hi, I am Brianna Wimer :)

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Ph.D. program for Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Notre Dame. My advisor is Ronald Metoyer. I am a member of the HCI Lab.

My research interests include understanding, designing, and developing technologies that support people with cognitive disabilities. My current projects involve making information visualizations more accessible to a wide variety of disabilities and designing self-support technology for adults with ADHD.

Yes, Sunny (fluffy white dog in the picture with me) is also in the PhD program. You can usually find him taking naps or begging for treats in my lab.
